About Nike

Nike is an American multinational company specializing in sportswear and footwear. Headquartered in Beaverton, Oregon. Nearly all Nike products are manufactured by third-party contractors outside of the United States, and the company itself owns trademarks, develops product designs and owns a chain of stores and NikeTown malls.
